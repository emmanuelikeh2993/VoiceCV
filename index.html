Frequently Asked Questions

Will this CV pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)?
Yes. Unlike complex PDF templates that confuse recruiting software, VoiceCV exports a clean, hierarchically structured document optimized for ATS parsers. We focus on keyword extraction, not flashy graphics.
Which languages are fully supported?
Our architecture perfectly handles English and Nigerian Pidgin. We have built the UI to scale for Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa, but are currently navigating the limitations of global Speech-to-Text APIs for local languages. Text input works universally.
Is my personal data stored?
No. Your voice and text data are processed entirely in-memory during the session. We do not store your personal details, email, or resume history in any database. Once you refresh the page, the data is gone.
